Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the home buying process usually take? +
The home buying process typically takes 14-30 days from offer acceptance to closing, but this can vary based on factors like financing, inspections, and negotiations. We work to make the process as efficient as possible for our clients.
What fees should I expect when selling my home? +
When selling a home, you can expect commission fees (typically 5-6% of the sale price), closing costs (1-3%), and potential repair or staging costs. We provide a detailed breakdown of all expected costs during our initial consultation.
Do you help with mortgage pre-approval? +
Yes, we work with several trusted mortgage lenders and can connect you with them for pre-approval. Getting pre-approved before house hunting strengthens your offer and helps you understand your budget.
How do you determine the value of my home? +
We use comparative market analysis (CMA) which looks at recent sales of similar properties in your area, current market conditions, and the specific features of your home. We also consider any upgrades or renovations you've made.
